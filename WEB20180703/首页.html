ABOUT ENVESTE

Enveste is a decentralized investment ecosystem that connects worldwide investments opportunities via crypto tokens. By applying the blockchain technology, Enveste has potential to solve the trust issues that hinder real world investment activities. Enveste sugguests a new way of investment - investing via crypto tokens. The new meothod has advantages, such as low transaction costs, international transferability, and pseudonymity, that are superior to investing via traditional currencies. Enveste removes the investment barriers and make the global investments opportunities more transparent and accessible to members of the Enveste community.
​At Enveste, we strive to create an efficient ecosystem that makes the investment borderless, easier and affordable for members of our community.
